About Us

We took the initiative to make a website exploring this topic because we feel that natural disasters are often left unattended and not majorly recognized for their results and the pain it causes. We, the students from 8H, believe that the people affected by natural disasters require more help, and more awareness on the topic should be spread around the world. We came to this conclusion when we realized that major parts of the world have recently seen destruction and chaos due to disasters such as earthquakes, tsunamis, and cyclones. People have been put out of their homes and their chance at life. This simulated website displays what is to be done to create a successful website to assist those who have been affected by natural disasters including a funds page, a page to contact us, and a page show where the most major disasters have taken place. Our collective dreams can only achieve so much, but if we can do so much as encourage the others around us, we move one step closer to a safer planet.
